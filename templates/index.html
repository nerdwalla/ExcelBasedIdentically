Operations

Identical.ly provides following operations to users. These include Identifying duplicates and analysis of the data.

Show Duplicates

This operation reads data from data source and presents any possible duplicates based on the all the business rules on either Persons or Organization type of customers.

Find Agents

This operation reads data from data source and finds all the agents who have duplicate customers

Find System

This operation reads data from data source and finds all the systems which have possibly added the duplicates into the data source.

Find Duplicates by customer type

Looks up the data set and illustrates the duplicate data by customer type

Benefits of identical.ly

  • Sanitized Data

    Sanitized data is always better data. Fewer errors make for happier clients and less-frustrated employees.

  • Time Saving

    Compared to existing manual process, automating deduplication process saves time of agents and other users.

  • Wasted budget

    Duplicate data results in poor data for marketing which in turn results in wasted budget for marketing.

  • Personalization

    Customers expect personalization and duplicate data hinders personalization.

  • Brand Perception

    Mistakes that come from duplicate data can harm the way that prospects and customers see our business.

  • Low-cost solution

    The smaller the storage capacity, the less expensive it is for licensing of data storage.

  • Data-backed decisions

    As any enterprise tries to become more data-driven and inject more data into its decision-making processes, Inaccurate data can cause skewed decision making

  • No single customer view

    78% of consumers will only engage with offers if they have been personalized, taking into account their previous interactions with a company.

  • Sales

    Having accurate data and a single customer view is critical for our agency teams as well as online portals.

  • Customer happiness

    Improving data quality by deduplication will result in happier customers.
